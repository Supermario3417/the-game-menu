Description

  • Welcome to Pong Masters: A simple pong game where you must score 7 to win!
  • - Play with 1 or 2 players with the Single Player and Double Player buttons
  • - Choose a bot difficulty from Easy, Normal, Hard, and Impossible (Which will always win)
  • - Play with a variety of music channels featuring 3417 (Yours truly), Skrillex, Imagine Dragons, and Alan Walker, who will be credited below!
  • Use the controls displayed in game to move and serve. First to seven wins.
  • This was a game I created in 2022, and decided to release it prior to my Pong Masters Pong Channel Album release. This is LITTERALLY my first game release, so I hope you enjoy!
